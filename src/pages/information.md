Arrival & Transportation Regulations (Group or Individual)
Compliance with NUSUK Regulations for Passenger Transportation
Visa Requirements
For both group and individual arrivals, passengers (Mumineen) must have visas issued by the same company if they wish to travel together.
As per NUSUK regulations, if passengers have visas from different companies, they must be transported in separate vehicles.
This rule applies to both groups and individuals.
Vehicle Allocation by Faiz
Faiz will arrange only one vehicle per registered group or individual, based on the arrival details provided. If an additional vehicle is required: A prior request must be submitted to Faiz, OR The tour operator or individual Zaereen must arrange their own transportation from the airport to Faiz. Additional vehicles are subject to availability, and extra charges will apply depending on the type of vehicle requested.
Responsibility for Penalties
If the individual Zaereen or tour operator does not request an additional vehicle when required (due to mixed visa companies), any penalties imposed by the authorities will be the responsibility of the tour operator or individual Zaereen. Please ensure full compliance to avoid delays, penalties, or inconvenience to the Mumineen.

Individuals can make New Applications uptil 24 hours before their arrival time at Saudia.
Individuals shall fill all the details in the registration form accurately.
Applications can be edited upto 48 Hours prior to the flight arrival time in saudia.
Though approved by Sigatulhaj, Approval Letter can be printed only after edit option is locked (48 hours prior to arrival time at Saudia).
If in case, after getting approval, Mumineen Zaereen Kiram could not come for Umrah & Ziarat for any reasons, Tour Operators / Individuals must send request through email for cancellation of their registration, prior to their arrival date, failing which would lead to blockage of their ITS IDs for any transaction on sigatulhaj website.

Zaereen shall take Sabaq from Raza na Saheb in their respective native places and also procure Mansak before coming for Umrah.

For FakkulEhram, groups are sent to haram twice a day.
1) 7.30 am in the morning &
2) 8 pm at night.
Zaereen coming uptil 1 hour before the said timings would be sent in that slot otherwise they would have to wait for the next slot.

Those groups planning for 2 umrahs shall make sure that they have minimum of 10 days gap between 2 umrahs. And after 2nd umrah zaereen must keep a day or 2 in spare before departing from Saudia.
